IEEE’s vision statement is:

Be essential to the global technical community and to technical professionals everywhere, and be universally recognized for the contributions of technology and of technical professionals in improving global conditions.

At UW-Stout, we strongly value the opportunity to integrate technology and engineering with professionalism through our Student Branch of the IEEE.  The benefits of local membership stretch far beyond the tangible elements, as we strive to build character and professional working relationships with industry professionals through diverse learning environments.

IEEE national membership offers many valuable incentives, as well.  These benefits include post-graduate career resources, access to the IEEE Xplore Digital Library and IEEE.tv, free subscriptions to highly-cited publications including IEEE Spectrum and IEEE Potentials, connection to world-wide conferences and events, and many more.  

Knowledge

  • IEEE members gain exclusive access to the latest and best research, news, television programs, and educational resources needed to expand their expertise and stay current.

Community

  • As an IEEE member, you are part of the world’s largest community of technology professionals—a network of communication and learning where innovators share what works, what does not, and what is needed now.

Profession

  • IEEE members can achieve more of what matters in your life and career through IEEE programs such as continuing education courses and certifications, conferences, competitions, giving or receiving mentoring, scholarships, and more.
